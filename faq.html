Frequently Asked Questions

How much does junk removal cost?

Pricing depends on the volume and type of items. We have a $95 minimum for all services. Contact us for a free quote - we'll give you an honest price based on what you need hauled.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes, same-day service is available when our schedule allows. Reach out early in the day for the best availability. For light demolition projects, we require at least 5 days advance notice.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Hudson, WI and the surrounding areas, including St. Croix County and the East Twin Cities Metro. Cities include River Falls, New Richmond, Stillwater, Woodbury, Cottage Grove, and more. See our full service area.

What items can you take?

We haul most household, commercial, and yard items including furniture, appliances, electronics, yard debris, and construction materials. See the full list on our About page.

What items DON'T you take?

For safety reasons, we cannot accept hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, propane, gasoline), batteries, asbestos, lead paint materials, medical waste, or ammunition. See the complete list.

Do you do full property cleanouts?

Yes! We handle entire home cleanouts, estate cleanouts, rental turnovers, foreclosures, and commercial property cleanouts. We work with realtors, property managers, and families handling estates. Learn more about full property cleanouts.

How do I book?

Just text or call 715-760-1610. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with a quote. Texting is the fastest way to reach us!

Do I need to be home during pickup?

It depends on the job. For simple pickups where items are accessible (driveway, curb, open garage), you may not need to be present. For interior work or property cleanouts, someone should be available. We'll coordinate this when scheduling.

What happens to my junk?

We sort items responsibly. Whenever possible, usable items are donated to local charities. Recyclable materials are recycled. The rest is disposed of properly at licensed facilities.
