About Unlimited Junk

What We Haul

We can haul away most household, commercial, and yard items, including:

  • Furniture - couches, mattresses, dressers, tables, chairs
  • Appliances - refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, dishwashers
  • Electronics - TVs, computers, monitors, printers
  • Yard debris - branches, brush, outdoor furniture
  • Construction debris - lumber, drywall, flooring (non-hazardous)
  • General household junk - boxes, toys, exercise equipment, and more

What We Don't Haul

For safety and regulatory reasons, we cannot accept:

  • Hazardous materials - paint, chemicals, solvents, propane tanks, gasoline
  • Batteries - car batteries, lithium batteries
  • Asbestos or lead paint materials
  • Medical waste - needles, pharmaceuticals, biohazard materials
  • Ammunition or firearms
